☕dalgona coffe

This yummy South Korean coffee treat that's all over the internet only requires four ingredients to make - all of which you probably have in the cupboard – you feel like you’ve won the jackpot, right?

ingredients

  • instant coffe
  • sugar
  • hot water
  • milk

making process

Step 1: Combine the instant coffee,suger,and hot water
Add 2 tablespoons(tbsp)of instant coffee,2 tbsp of sugar, and 2tbsp of hot water to a medium mixing bowl.
Step 2: Whisk, whisk, whisk until the coffee is light and fluffy
Using an electric hand-held mixer,whip the coffee mixture until it is light brown, fluffy, and holds stiff peaks when whisk is removed.
Serve with milk:
Use a spoon to granish a glass of hot or cold milk with coffee cream, server with straw, and stir,stir ,stir to enjoy!
